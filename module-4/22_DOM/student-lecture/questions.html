A Friendly Note

Don't worry if you can't give great answers to all or even most of these. Aim for being able to successfully answer around 70% or less of the questions asked of you.

Focus on conveying a good impression as someone responsible, competent, and able to learn and grow.

Remember that people are looking for someone who is:

  • Professional, Responsible, and Reliable
  • Knows how to program enough to work without constant help (regular check-ins are normal and expected)
  • Able and willing to continue to learn and grow
